Honda Civic Fl5 Type R

The Honda Civic Type R FL5, or the fifth generation of the Civic Type R, continues the legacy of its predecessors with enhancements in performance, design, and technology.

This high-performance vehicle is equipped with a turbocharged 2.0-liter inline-4 engine delivering approximately 316 horsepower (HP) and 295 lb-ft of torque. Power is transferred through a responsive 6-speed manual transmission, enabling rapid acceleration from 0 to 60 mph in about 5.5 seconds, with a top speed reaching around 169 mph (272 km/h).

Toyota GR86

The GR86 is a sports car developed jointly by Toyota and Subaru, known for its agility, balance, and driver-focused performance.

The GR86, a Toyota-Subaru collaboration, packs a Subaru 2.4L boxer engine with 228 HP and 184 lb-ft torque. Choose between a 6-speed manual or optional 6-speed automatic with paddle shifters. It hits 0-60 mph in 6.1 seconds (manual) and tops out at 140 mph. With MacPherson strut front, double wishbone rear suspension, ventilated disc brakes, and lightweight alloy wheels with high-performance tires, it offers agile handling and strong braking. Designed for enthusiasts, it blends dynamic performance and sleek aerodynamics for a thrilling drive.

Toyota Supra MK5

The Toyota Supra MK5, or the A90 Supra, is a modern sports car that continues the legacy of Toyota's iconic Supra line.

The Toyota Supra MK5, or A90 Supra, features a BMW-sourced 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 engine producing around 382 horsepower and 368 lb-ft of torque. Paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission with paddle shifters, it accelerates from 0 to 60 mph in about 3.9 seconds, with a top speed electronically limited to 155 mph. Its suspension includes a double-joint type MacPherson strut at the front and a multi-link setup at the rear.
