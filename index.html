Honda Civic Fl5 Type R

The Honda Civic Type R FL5, or the fifth generation of the Civic Type R, continues the legacy of its predecessors with enhancements in performance, design, and technology.

This high-performance vehicle is equipped with a turbocharged 2.0-liter inline-4 engine delivering approximately 316 horsepower (HP) and 295 lb-ft of torque.
